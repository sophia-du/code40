# Case Specifications
- 8 layer stacked case
## 3D Printed Layers
- **Layers**: 1, 3, 4, 8
- Plate is optional but recommended for 3 pin switches for stability
- **Print Settings**: 0.20 mm speed, 15% infill on Prusa MK3S
## Acrylic Layers
- **Layers**: 2, 5, 6, 7
- Laser cut
- 3 mm clear acrylic
